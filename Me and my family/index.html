My Education
 My education background is that I graduated from Ningbo Foreign Affairs School in high school. During high school, I studied culture and various specialized courses carefully, got along well with classmates and teachers, and actively participated in various activities organized by the school. I also won the second prize of the school photography contest and the first prize of Ningbo Popularizing Law Video Shooting. I'm ready to go to college now, and I'm looking forward to going to college to study art design. My teacher is very strict, but he taught me a lot of knowledge about designing software, and I am looking forward to becoming an excellent designer through study!
My hobbies
My hobbies are painting, photography, dancing and so on. I can draw sketches, gouache and oil paintings. What I like most is to paint landscapes with colorful paints. It is very meaningful to paint landscapes on paper with brush strokes and gives me a sense of accomplishment. I am also very interested in photography, I will use the camera to record all kinds of things in my life, after shooting, I will edit the photo, adjust its saturation, contrast and so on, to make it look more perfect, and then I will share the photo on my social platform for my friends to see together. I learned Latin dance since kindergarten and got the certificate of Level 10. But now I like Kpop music, so I will learn to dance some Kpop dance. Dancing enriches my spare time and exercises my body at the same time. These hobbies are an essential part of my life and I hope I can always love them!
My carrer
I am a senior high school student. I will do various volunteer work in winter and summer vacation, such as working as a librarian in the library, cleaning garbage in the street, and sometimes I will go to the studio to teach children how to draw.
